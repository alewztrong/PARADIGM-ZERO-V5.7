O Achado de Andersen et al. (2025)
O paper do EGRIP (East Greenland Ice-core Project) demonstra que:

"Cold stadial periods consistently exhibit greater variability than warm interstadial periods. Most notably, shifts in interannual-scale climatic variability lead Dansgaard–Oeschger abrupt warming by hundreds of years."

A amplitude da variabilidade de 7–15 anos no δ¹⁸O aumenta centenas de anos antes do aquecimento abrupto
